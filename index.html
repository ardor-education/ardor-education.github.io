Ardor Concept

Ardor is dedicated to helping people learn mathematics.

Ardor Education, Inc. is a software education company building tools that leverage technology, to increase student performance and improve teacher efficiency. At Ardor we believe that teachers should focus on their passion of teaching students; not doing paper work, and students should be excited about learning. With Ardor teaching tools and adaptive math curriculum we are transforming the classroom experience.
