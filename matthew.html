I firmly believe that the blend of creative teaching with creatively designed software is the solution to our education dilemma. I want to create a cultural revolution by creating generations of students who love Mathematics. I am very fortunate that I am now able to pursue my blended passions of Mathematics, Software, and Education. I am excited to see the future that the team here at Ardor will create.
