About Us

The current generation of students has grown up with technology, and they expect technology to work for them. we are leveraging technology to build mathematic curriculum that is interactive and adaptive to the learner. The Ardor experience is unique for each learner. Ardor adjusts the difficulty of the math concept to challenge or remediate, while providing valuable feedback and incentives.
